## ACTIVITY LABELS

1 = WALKING Test subject was walking when measurement was performed

2 = WALKING_UP Test subject was walking up stairs when measurement was performed

3 = WALKING_DOWN Test subject was walking down stairs when measurement was performed

4 = SITTING Test subject was sitting when measurement was performed

5 = STANDING Test subject was standing when measurement was performed

6 = LAYING Test subject was laying down when measurement was performed
